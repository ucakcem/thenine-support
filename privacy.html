The Nine

Privacy Policy

Last updated: July 2026

Overview

The Nine ("the app") is a Sudoku game for iOS. We do not collect, store, or share your personal data on our servers. The app does not contact any servers we operate.

Data Stored on Your Device

The app stores your settings (haptics, conflict highlighting, theme), your progress (unlocks, completion counts, best scores, best times, score history), your daily puzzle streak, and your in-progress game on your device using iOS standard storage (UserDefaults). None of this data is sent to us or to any third party for our own use.

iCloud Sync

If you enable "iCloud Sync" in Settings, the app uses Apple's iCloud Key-Value Storage to sync your unlocks, scores and daily streak between your devices signed into the same Apple ID. Your in-progress game stays on the device it was started on. This sync is operated entirely by Apple; we do not have access to it. You can turn iCloud Sync off in Settings at any time, and you can clear all progress (locally and from iCloud) using "Reset Progress" in Settings.

Game Center

The app integrates with Apple's Game Center to submit scores to leaderboards. Whether you sign into Game Center, and what is shared, is controlled by Apple and by your Game Center settings on your device. We do not collect or store your Game Center player ID. If Game Center is unavailable or you decline to sign in, the app continues to work normally; only leaderboards are skipped.

Analytics & Advertising

We do not use third-party analytics, tracking, or advertising in the app. The app does not track your activity across other apps or websites.

Contact

For privacy-related questions or requests, contact us at ucak@outlook.com.

Changes

We may update this privacy policy from time to time. The "Last updated" date at the top will be revised when we do. Continued use of the app after changes means you accept the updated policy.
